Our Heritage

Our Story

Family Tradition Since Generations

We are a family-owned farm nestled in the fertile Neretva Valley, where generations of the Oršulić family have cultivated the land with passion and dedication. Our valley's unique microclimate and rich soil create the perfect conditions for growing exceptional citrus fruits and vegetables.

For decades, we've been committed to natural farming practices, producing the finest mandarins, lemons, and handcrafted goods. Every product that leaves our farm carries the authentic taste of Neretva and the care of our family's hands.

Our mission is simple: to bring the pure, sun-kissed flavors of the Neretva Valley directly to your table, maintaining the highest standards of quality and sustainability.

Our Signature Product

Neretvanska Mandarin Juice

Perfected through years of dedicated production, our mandarin juice represents the culmination of generational expertise and unwavering commitment to quality.

  • 100% Pure Mandarin Juice
  • 0% Added Sugar
  • 0% Preservatives
  • Cold-Pressed from 3-4 kg of Hand-Picked Mandarins per Liter
  • Harvested at Peak Ripeness
  • Traditional Production Methods
  • Available in 1L Bottles

For Retail Partners: Our mandarin juice is the result of multi-generational farming expertise combined with modern quality standards. Each batch undergoes rigorous quality control, ensuring consistency and exceptional taste. We offer competitive wholesale pricing, reliable supply chain management, and full traceability from orchard to bottle. Perfect for health-conscious consumers seeking authentic, premium citrus products.

The Neretva Advantage

Mandarins grown in Neretva's unique soil contain 3-4x more polyphenols and antioxidants than mandarins from any other region. This exceptional nutrient density is what makes our cold-pressed elixir more powerful, healthier, and richer in authentic taste. Every bottle captures the pure essence of the Neretva Valley's mineral-rich earth.
